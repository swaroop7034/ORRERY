About Us

part of the NASA Space Apps Challenge, Kepler Knights is developing an interactive and dynamic web-based orrery that visually represents the motion and position of celestial bodies, including planets, Near-Earth Asteroids (NEAs), Near-Earth Comets (NECs), and Potentially Hazardous Asteroids (PHAs). Our orrery aims to educate and engage users by offering an immersive, 3D experience that leverages real-time NASA data. Through this app, users will be able to explore the solar system, track asteroid movements, and learn about the potential dangers and opportunities these space objects present. Whether it's simulating asteroid mining missions or safely diverting PHAs, our orrery delivers a visually stunning, scientifically accurate, and highly interactive experience.
